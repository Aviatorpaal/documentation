TrueCommand 1.1 has reached its End of Life and is no longer receiving security updates.
The TrueCommand 1.3.2 release announcement can be found at https://www.ixsystems.com/blog/truecommand-1-3-2/.

Please schedule a time to upgrade to the latest version of TrueCommand. If assistance is required, please contact the [iXsystems Support Team]({{< relref "/hub/initial-setup/support.md#contacting-ixsystems-support" >}}).
